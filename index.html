About us

Welcome To Paisano Group

Paisano in Italian means someone from the same town. It is also used as a slang word for old friend. This is what the Paisano Group is all about.

Our mission is to treat our customers as we would an old precious friends and therefore provide hospitality as we would in our own homes. This means greeting all who walk through our doors with a friendly face, personalised service, fabulous fresh quality foods, delicious coffee and hot and cold beverages. We welcome and encourage our guests to visit us again.

Paisano Espresso

Located in the foyer of the Telstra building at 69 Ann Street, Brisbane, allow yourself to experience good old fashioned customer service in a comfortable yet contemporary space.

Enjoy a cup of freshly made Vittoria coffee, or if you prefer, try something a little different such as a flavoured latte or a doppio (double shot, extracted using a double coffee filter in the portafilter), for those days you just need that extra boost. Combine a coffee with a special sweet treat. At Paisano Espresso we provide a wide selection of freshly made cakes and slices, perfect for the mid-morning or afternoon sweet tooth.

Popping in for lunch? Choose from an array of freshly made sandwiches, wraps, rolls and focaccias - all made with only the highest quality produce and meats. Or perhaps choose one of our tasty salads, dine in, in the air-conditioned comfort or enjoy your meal in the sunshine in our outdoor space.

Friendly staff, quality fresh foods and a relaxed environment is all on offer at Paisano Espresso. Drop in and enjoy!

Chocolate Soldier

Mmmm...chocolate. There's nothing quite like it that stimulates all of our senses and provides that mood and mind-altering pleasure. Just hearing the snap of a block, biting into a crisp handmade truffle and feeling that ooze of creamy filling, smelling your favourite freshly baked cake or simply enjoying a warming mug of chocolate can turn your day around and bring you to your happy place.

At Chocolate Soldier Espresso Bar in Brisbane, we're purveyors and lovers of fine handmade chocolate, and we love sharing our passion and knowledge with everyone who walks through our doors. Whatever our customers seek in their food or beverage, it's our mission to ensure they leave completely satisfied by their experience and maybe even thrilled by discovering something new to enjoy!

Located on 400 George Street in Brisbane's CBD, Chocolate Soldier Espresso Bar attracts chocolate lovers from all walks of life. Whether you're catching up with a friend, enjoying some alone time with a good read, having a meeting or taking a break from the shops and sights of the city, Chocolate Soldier Espresso Bar is conveniently accessible. Pop in and grab something to go, or linger as long as you please.

Comfort for the mind and soul is the key the minute you step into Chocolate Soldier Espresso Bar. Our dark and light wooden interiors from wall panels to tables and chairs bring warmth and cosiness. Whether you're craving something savoury or sweet, you won't be disappointed. Our wraps and sandwiches are made on the day and are equally enjoyable fresh or toasted.

If you need to be spoilt, our cakes, tarts and biscuits in a myriad of flavours especially chocolate varieties will hit the spot. Our signature offerings include a divine handmade truffles and other chocolate delights, inspired by Australian and international flavour palettes, ranging in texture and intensity. Enjoy in our café or have your handmade selection beautifully packaged and presented as a gift.

Our knowledgeable and friendly staff will be more than happy to assist you with your selection and provide food and beverage pairing suggestions for the most complementary experience.

Espresso Veloce

Translated into English, Espresso Veloce means fast coffee. From brisk service and constant flow of patrons along the streamlined counter, everything about Espresso Veloce espresso bar encapsulates a speedy pace against an energetic atmosphere. Espresso Veloce brings a slice of Italy's bustling street cafes for Brisbane's corporate set to enjoy.

Located in the foyer of the prestigious 10 Eagle St business tower, a stone's throw from the Queen Street mall, Eagle Street dining strip and botanic gardens, Espresso Veloce cafe lives true to its name by being amidst the dynamic buzz of Brisbane's CBD.

Step inside Espresso Veloce into a refined space of cool marble floors that set off the sleek white backdrop framing the service bar. The aroma of espresso beans being freshly ground in whirring machines at the espresso bar immediately awakens the senses, while a tempting array of delicacies in the glass display beckon you for a closer look.

Whether for a breakfast meeting, a quick pick-me-up before the workday begins or a lunch catch-up with the team or clients, Espresso Veloce provides the perfect comfortable spot for business and social meetings.

From small bites to something more substantial, we provide a mouth-watering range of breakfast and lunch items. Macaroons are the perfect small sweet treat, and our pastries including classic favourites like croissants are popular choices. Featuring freshly baked breads in-house, a selection of meats, cheeses and salads, our sandwiches are a heartier option whether you want something healthy or more decadent. And if you have trouble choosing just one delicious morsel, our antipasto platters are excellent for sharing.

Espresso Veloce specialises in Corporate Catering. Call us for your next event, and we'll discuss your requirements and devise a customised menu that suits your preferences. For your peace of mind, we cater to vegetarians, Celiac sufferers who are gluten-intolerant and those with other allergies.
